Case Study: Immersive AR product presentations - Client: Samsung Milan.

Prototype was created in 2018 with WebARCore(deprecated*) library.
You can find the technical documentation here.
On desktop it's possible to see objects in 3d environments on mobile was in augmented reality*.

"Samsung DeX allows you to connect your Galaxy S8 / S8 +, Note8 or S9 / S9 + device to a monitor, keyboard and mouse for a desktop experience via your phone. In this way, you can transform any station into a workplace."

* Durante l'utilizzo di Samsung DeX, sul display del dispositivo mobile si attiva la modalità di standby.
Step 1/3: Connetti il tuo Samsung Galaxy S8/S8+/Note8 alla DeX Station.
Step 2/3: È sufficiente collegare il cavo HDMI alla DeX Station per connettere il monitor.
Step 3/3: Connetti Mouse e Tastiera tramite Bluetooth.
